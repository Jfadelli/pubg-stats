Good posture helps you be more productive, improves your mood, is healthier for your body, and makes you look like a million bucks. Start with aligning your body and then build your workspace up to support your new upright and balanced posture.

Is Your Posture In Line With What You Are Doing?

 

Reclined posture is perfect for passive activities like reading, watching videos, or relaxing.

 

Forward and supported posture is great for activities like writing, drawing, creating to-do lists, and even doodling.

 

Upright balanced posture is best for working with a computer, laptop or doing desktop work.
